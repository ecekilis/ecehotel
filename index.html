OUR ROOMS

Deluxe Rooms

From a furnished balcony or terrace you can enjoy beautiful views of the pools, the lazy river or the lush gardens. Additional amenities include a separate work area and seating area. There is also a marble bathroom with a walk-in shower.

Standard Rooms

The 22-24 square meter rooms of the standard category are located at the back of the house and offer the highest level of sleeping and living comfort. All standard rooms have air conditioning, a spacious desk, underfloor heating in the bathroom and a modern bathtub.

Family Rooms

This family suite has a bedroom with a king-size bed, a separate bedroom with 2 single beds for children and an elegant marble bathroom with a walk-in shower. The beds are made with soft Egyptian cotton sheets.
